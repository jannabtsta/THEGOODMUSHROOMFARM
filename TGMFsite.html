 Farm Equipments

      
      

      

      
 FARM EQUIPMENTs play a crucial role in our mushroom farming by significantly increasing our efficiency, productivity, and overall output.
        These help save our time and energy; reduce the need for more manual labor and physical strain on farmers.

      

      

        
          
          
        
      



      

        
        

        
Substrate Mixer

        
-- It is designed to blend or mix different 
          ingredients with the substrates to create a uniform and well-balanced mixture for planting or cultivating mushrooms.
 

        
        

          
Autoclave

        
 -- Our autoclave is a product of innovation. 
          It is a pressurized device used for sterilization fruiting bag to high-temperature steam, typically around 
          121°C (250°F) or higher. This controlled heat and pressure environment effectively eliminates bacteria, 
          viruses, spores, and other microorganisms.
 


          

          
Bag Press

          
 -- It is a mechanical device we 
            designed to compress or squeeze substrates within a fruiting bag to achieve desired height and weight. 
            It applies pressure to the bag's contents.
